Chi Liu

 

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Pricision Instrument at Tsinghua University and advised by Lingjie Kong. My current research interests focus on multi-photon imaging. I am interested in developing new imaging system and setup related to high speed two photon imaging and two photon optogenetics technique.

I received a B.E. degree in Pricision Instrument and a minor degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China in 2019.
